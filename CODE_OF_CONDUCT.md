# Code of Conduct

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Prioritizing the community and project health.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, issues, pull requests, and community discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainer.
